# SCRAM v3.1 — Stabilisation & Deployment Guardrails

Deployed: 22 March 2026
Freeze ends: 5 April 2026

---

## Freeze Rule

No major structural or copy changes for 14 days after deployment.

Allowed: bug fixes, broken layout fixes, image/render fixes.

Not allowed: new sections, CTA changes, additional proof blocks, pricing introduction.

---

## Delivery Rule

All audits must be delivered within 24 hours of submission.

---

## Tracking Log

Use this format to log every submission:

| Date | Name / Business | Website submitted | Stated problem | Actual problem (diagnosis) | Audit sent | Response received | Converted to project |
|------|-----------------|-------------------|----------------|---------------------------|------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| | | | | | Y/N | Y/N | Y/N |

---

## After 10 Submissions — Quality Check

1. Are people sending real business websites?
2. Do they understand what they are getting?
3. Are submissions aligned with your services?

If quality is high: keep system as is.
If quality is low: refine contact page wording only (not homepage).

---

## After 10–20 Audits — Pattern Extraction

Identify:
- Top 3 recurring issues
- Common industries
- Repeated misunderstandings

Turn into: future content, refined messaging, stronger proof blocks.

---

## Conversion Signal — Ready to Evolve

You are ready for v3.2 when:
- Users submit without hesitation
- Audits are fast to deliver
- At least 20–30% lead to conversation
- Some convert into paid work

---

## Do Not Optimise Prematurely

Avoid: changing CTA again, adding pricing early, adding more sections, over-explaining the offer.

Optimise based on behaviour, not opinion.

---

## v3.2 Trigger

Move forward only when:
- 20+ audits completed
- Patterns clearly repeat
- Delivery feels easy

v3.2 will include: optional pricing (£49), audit productisation, faster delivery systems.
