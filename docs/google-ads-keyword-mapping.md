## Keyword Density Guidelines

### Target Ranges
- **Primary Keyword:** 1-2% of total content
- **Secondary Keywords:** 0.5-1% each
- **Location Keywords:** 2-3 mentions per page
- **LSI Keywords:** Natural distribution

### Avoid
- ❌ Keyword stuffing (>3% density)
- ❌ Unnatural keyword placement
- ❌ Exact match repetition
- ❌ Hidden text or keyword cloaking

---

## Heading Structure Audit

### Best Practices
- ✅ One H1 per page (primary keyword)
- ✅ H2s for main sections (secondary keywords)
- ✅ H3s for subsections (LSI keywords)
- ✅ Logical hierarchy (no skipping levels)

## Quality Score Optimization

### Factors Affecting Quality Score

1. **Expected CTR** (35% weight)
   - Compelling ad copy
   - Relevant keywords
   - Strong call-to-action

2. **Ad Relevance** (35% weight)
   - Keyword in ad headline
   - Keyword in ad description
   - Keyword in landing page H1

3. **Landing Page Experience** (30% weight)
   - Fast load time (LCP < 2.5s) ✅
   - Mobile-friendly ✅
   - Relevant content ✅
   - Clear CTA ✅
   - Easy navigation ✅
